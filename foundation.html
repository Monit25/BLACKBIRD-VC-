Our mission is to unleash creativity in young people. A young person who is creative is confident, comfortable with the unknown and a curious, life long learner. These are key attributes needed to sail the open seas and confront an uncertain future with increasingly complex challenges. Creativity is the core capability needed to navigate a world of increasing uncertainty, and we believe it should be cultivated in all young people.

Unleashing Creativity

We believe creativity is the metaskill that makes everything possible. It unlocks potential and can lead to a life full of meaning, richness and purpose. To unleash creativity, we fund individuals, organisations, and tell stories.

01

Protostars is our microgrant program for young people with passion projects. Visit our online exhibition from Season 1, learn more about the Protostars program, and check out the latest passion projects from Season 2.

Protostars
02

We provide strategic, multi-year, untied funding to organisations who are working to unleash creativity in young people. At this stage in our journey, we can fund 1-2 organisations per year. If you’re doing this work, get in touch.
