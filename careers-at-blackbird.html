Life At Blackbird

We are building a team that is as ambitious as the companies we invest in.

We want Blackbird to be the best place to work in Australia & New Zealand.

In 2021, we were recognised by the AFR for Best Employee Experience in Australia, (which was pretty exciting) but what we really care about is our culture - and that’s stayed steady at a 96% engagement score on CultureAmp.

In two years, our team has grown from 12 to 70 people, and we’re only just getting started.

Our Northstar and Values

Giant leaps forward made by a passionate few 🚀🚀🚀

Giant leaps forward: We believe in a future where humanity continues to leap forward, to overcome our great challenges.

Passionate few: Blackbird was founded on the belief that small groups of people create the biggest change. The name “Blackbird” comes from a jet developed by Lockheed Martin’s Skunk Works team, who were separated out from the main engineering organisation to design and build jets that were inconceivable to most.

1.

We wear T-Shirts.

Blackbird is a startup and will always be run as a startup. Our style, our office, our events and our communications relate to the founders we back.

2.

We are true believers in founders.

Founders come first, they are our reason for being. We work for our founders, they don’t work for us.

3.

We believe in love at first sight.

We make high conviction decisions after careful research and debate, but also trust our instincts built up over years of pattern matching.

4.

We think in decades not days.

We invest for the long term. Building iconic businesses takes the best part of a decade  or longer. We don’t plan for exits.

5.

We are comfortable with the misunderstood.

We don’t need to know all the answers to make an investment decision. We’ll look to maximise the upside, rather than minimise the risks.

6.

We will make our kids proud.

Everything we do, we do with integrity, in a way that we’d be happy to tell our kids about.

Our Hiring Process

We don’t discriminate on the basis of race, religion, national origin, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, age, or marital, veteran, or disability status. We are proud to do blind hiring through Applied, one of our portfolio companies, to ensure every applicant gets an equal chance to succeed.

1.

Applied

We use a blind application process through Applied. You'll answer 4-5 questions relating to the skills we're looking for.

2.

Phone Call

We contact the top applicants from Applied for a 15 minute chat about the role and what you want to get out of it.

3.

Interview

We conduct 2-3 interviews in person (or on Zoom) to get to know you better.

4.

References

We talk to a few of your references to get a feel for what it's like to work with you.

5.

Offer

We make an offer to the candidate we feel is the best fit for the role.

Benefits At Blackbird

We’re building Blackbird like a startup, not a traditional VC firm. That means deep work, planning and thoughtful discussion is important, but so is having fun. There’s weekly team lunches, good coffee, better music (not to mention resident DJ Lil’ Butter) and all kinds of social clubs from running to reading fantasy books.

01

We’re flexible by default with working locations and hours to suit your life, whether it’s working from home or leaving early to pick up the kids from daycare or school.

Work on Your Terms
02

We’re proud of our employee-first policies, from 20+ weeks of parental leave, to our quarterly KitKat days where our entire team takes a break with a full office shutdown.

Family First
03

Happiness and work satisfaction is everything. We survey the team regularly to measure wellness and engagement and are proud to say we score over 90% on both!

Measuring Happiness
04

We work hard without letting work get in the way of life. We’ve implemented no Slack or emails after hours or on weekends, so you can switch off and enjoy yourself.

Unplug and Relax
05

Our team is hungry to continuously learn and you’ll receive an annual $5000 budget to spend on whatever you’d like to learn - from coding courses to meditation.

Become a Learn-it-All
06

We believe every Blackbird contributes as an owner of our company and deserves to be recognised as one. All of our team have carried interest and are owners in the management company, to share in our financial upside.
