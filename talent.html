Welcome to the Lilypad

The Lilypad connects talent from our collective networks and all walks of life with the hiring teams at Blackbird portfolio startups.

We want the hungry and the proven. You do not need to have worked in a startup before, or you might be a tech scaling veteran ready for a change. It’s free to join, so if you want to leapfrog into a startup, all you need to do is sign up.

SIGN UP TO THE LILYPAD
01

Through the Lilypad, you can be searched and get directly introduced to the founders and hiring managers for our 100+ portfolio companies. So far 50+ talented people have landed epic roles from Heads of Product to Engineering Managers from Marketing to Sales.

Connect with our companies
02

Our startups are always on the lookout for great talent and through the Lilypad, they’re able to connect with you for exciting roles before they are even on the market. Using the Lilypad, our companies have secured hard-to-fill roles across all functions from Engineering to Sales, to Data Science and even Talent!

Get exclusive opportunities
03

When you join the Lilypad you’ll be able to nominate the companies, what stage or industries you’re most interested in joining, and show up to their hiring teams. And with the Blackbird portfolio growing all the time, you’ll be kept up to date with new companies to explore.

Select your favourite startups and meet new ones
04

There’s over 1000+ ambitious people in the Lilypad community, including talent from Facebook, Google, Amazon and Atlassian and legends from all around the world. Join our community to get first access to our talent program, exclusive event invites, content and more.

Our People Programs at Blackbird

There’s no bigger impact on a startup’s success than the people it hires and that’s why we’re obsessed with helping our portfolio companies build the best teams in the world. Here’s some of our current programs to get more talented people into startups.

01

The Blackbird Sales Academy is a free, six-week community program up-skilling and connecting ambitious people to sales roles in our fastest-growing startups. Your background is less important than your attitude to succeed, so if you’re working in any customer-centric role and craving a change, this program is for you.

learn more
Sales Academy
02

We’ve custom-built a free, fully-virtual work experience program with Forage to help get more computer science graduates into startups. Learn what it is like on the inside, and gain the practical experience and confidence to leap into a career in software engineering in a startup.

learn more
Forage Virtual Work Experience
03

We help our companies attract the best global leadership and tech talent in the world. If you’re a seasoned tech executive looking for your next opportunity, our People Lead Nell would love to give you a steer on the juiciest leadership opportunities in our portfolio - many of which are not advertised.

learn more
Supercharging Executive Talent
04

Hundreds of ambitious women have found their dream job in a startup through the Startmate Women Fellowship, a two-month program helping women gain the tools, connections and confidence to hit the ground running in a fast-paced startup.
