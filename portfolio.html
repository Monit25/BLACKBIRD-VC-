Why Founders Choose Blackbird

We back wild hearts doing their life’s work and tackling the world’s greatest problems. Your idea could be anything from software to space, unified only by the biggest of ambitions. Here’s how we’ll help you succeed.

Drag Me!
01

Our whole team works for you, not just your lead investor. From specialist advice to 1:1 support and introductions, you can count on any Blackbird to lend a hand on your journey.

1:1 and 1:many
02

We create products to get thousands more people to find their way into startups. From connecting you to board advisors to finding your next executive hire, we combine our ecosystem programs and 1:1 support to bring the best people into your orbit.

Helping you build your team
03

Our community of founders come together with special programs, events and content. Get advice from the group in real-time, spend quality time together at our Founder Retreat, and join special programming tackling your thorniest questions.
