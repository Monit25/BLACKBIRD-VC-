Joining the Blackbird Flock

Now the world's best companies are founded outside of Silicon Valley, the world's best venture firms are too. Our portfolio is worth over $7B, including some of the most successful Aussie and Kiwi-founded startups such as Canva, Zoox, SafetyCulture and Culture Amp. But the numbers only tell part of the story. Here’s what makes us different.

01

Since 2012, we have backed more than 100 companies across Australia and New Zealand: 25% of those are now worth over $100M and six are worth over $1B.

Top Quartile Returns
02

At the heart of our LP base are successful tech founders surrounded by great institutions supporting millions of Australians and New Zealanders. Our LP community includes the founders of Atlassian, Aconex, Campaign Monitor, Trade Me and more.

Founder-Powered Flywheel
